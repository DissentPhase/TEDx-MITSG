About SRE UNHAS

Society of Renewable Energy Indonesia Chapter Universitas Hasanuddin (SRE UNHAS) is a student-led organization based in Universitas Hasanuddin which concerns about new and renewable energy and committed to advancing new and renewable energy in Indonesia.

About SICLUS

This event features the grand theme "Visioning a City of the Future: Pioneering Innovations in Infrastructure, Affordable Energy Solutions, Green Mobility, and Circular Material Flows to Create a Future-Ready Metropolis." This year, SICLUS 2024 will operate in a hybrid format, with the Student Bootcamp, Innovation Paper Competition, Case Study, Talk Show, and awarding ceremonies conducted in-person, while the Photography Competition will be held online.
